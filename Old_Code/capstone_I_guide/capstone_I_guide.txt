CAPSTONE I GUIDE 

PROPOSAL
First, you need to write a proposal and get your project ok'd by the instructors.
Your proposal should contain:

1. Your question
2. A description of your data AND evidence that you have looked at it
3. The Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

It should be 1 page (a little over/under ok), exported to pdf, and named 
first_last_proposal_1.pdf

We will provide a Dropbox link to upload your proposal.

It will be due in the Dropbox folder Thursday, May 23rd by 9 am.

PRESENTATION OF RESULTS
You'll be presenting your results from your README.md in ~ 10 minute presentation
Friday, May 31st.
